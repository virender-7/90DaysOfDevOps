- What is your understanding of DevOps and Cloud Engeering?
**DevOps**
> **Collaboration and Shared Ownership**: Developers and operations teams work as a unified team. Devs take ownership of how code performs in production, while Ops get involved early in the design phase.
> **Automation**: Manual processes are replaced with automated workflows, allowing for continuous, reliable software releases.
> **Continuous Integration & Continuous Delivery (CI/CD)**: Code changes are frequently and automatically built, tested, and prepped for release.

**Cloud Engeering** is an IT professional responsible for desinging, building, and maintaining infrastructure. They manage remote computing system (like Utho, GCP, Azure, or AWS)
to ensure applications are scalable, secure, and highly.
